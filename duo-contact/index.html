DUO is a self made company located on the beautiful beaches of Hawaii. Located very close to the famously known Banzai Pipeline or, better known as "Pipeline", many of our surfers go on to surf some of the biggest and best waves in the world. Don't be too scared by the pro's over at Pipeline though, almost all of our students start with little to no experience! We aim to help out students become self sufficent surfers and then help them improve to the desired level. Many of our students have gone on to be ranking surfers in competitions around the world!

Surfing not neccesarily your thing? No problem! Duo offers a beach front Yoga program for all ages!

Here at DUO, we treat the practice of yoga with the upmost respect. With our world renound instructors ranging in every style of yoga practice, we are confident you will be satisfied with your yoga experience!
